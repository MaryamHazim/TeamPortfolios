Hello everyone, my name is Maryam. I am an 18-year-old first-year university student who is passionate about creativity and learning new things. I chose IT Multimedia because it combines creativity and technology, especially in areas like design and digital content creation. In my free time, I enjoy drawing, as it helps me relax and express my thoughts, as well as crochet and embroidery, which teach me patience and attention to detail. I also like learning new languages such as Italian, Japanese, and Turkish to connect with different cultures. My goal is to build a career in multimedia or digital design, and I believe that consistency and hard work will help me achieve that.
