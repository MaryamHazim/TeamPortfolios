Shaima Al Edresi

IT Multimedia student

I’m Shaima Shaif Al-Edresi, an IT Multimedia student passionate about combining technology and creativity. As a Muslim, my faith guides me to live with sincerity, discipline, and purpose, and I stand for truth, justice, kindness, and self-improvement. In my free time, I enjoy drawing both traditionally and digitally, along with painting, Arabic calligraphy, and henna, often while listening to podcasts. These hobbies help me build creativity, patience, and attention to detail. My goals are to graduate with a 4.0 GPA and memorize the Qur’an, and I work toward them through consistency, discipline, and trust in Allah.
